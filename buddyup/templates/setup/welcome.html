Welcome to BuddyUp!

BuddyUp is a website created by students, for students.

It's designed to help you find study buddies and organize face-to- face study groups. Research shows that students who participate in study groups are more likely to succeed academically.

Take a few moments to set up your profile and then go exploring.

Any ideas or suggestions? Please email Brian Forrester at willbforrester@gmail.com .

You're among the first users and your engagement is strongly encouraged! Help yourself, help your peers and make friends.
