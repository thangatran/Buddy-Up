Welcome to BuddyUp!

BuddyUp is a website created by students, for students. It’s designed to help you find study buddies and organize face-to- face study groups. Research shows that students who participate in study groups are more likely to succeed academically. Take a few moments to set up your profile and then go exploring. Any ideas or suggestions?
